### Bio Formatting Rules:
- Each line should be on its OWN line (use line breaks, not continuous text)
- To get line breaks on Instagram: type your bio in your Notes app first, then paste it in

## 3. PROFILE PICTURE

### Direction: Custom Logo (NOT a face photo)
You are a curator brand, not a personal brand. Your profile pic must be a **logo mark**.

### Design Brief:
- **Background:** Dark/black (like FLOW's logo) — stands out in feeds and story rings
- **Text:** "YMP" monogram OR the full words "You Must Pray" in a modern, bold sans-serif font
- **Style:** Minimalist, premium, clean — think Netflix, FLOW, Hillsong-level design
- **Color palette:**
  - Primary: White text on dark background
  - Accent: Gold OR deep red (blood of Christ / fire of the Holy Spirit)
- **Do NOT use:** Clip art, stock crosses, hands in prayer emoji-style art, or anything that looks like a 2010 church flyer

### Logo Options to Consider:
```
Option 1: Dark circle with "YMP" in bold white serif font
Option 2: Dark circle with praying hands silhouette + "You Must Pray" text
Option 3: Dark circle with a subtle flame/fire element + "YMP"
Option 4: Dark background with just "You Must Pray." in elegant white text (like FLOW's "flow." logo)
```

### Recommended: Option 4
Mirror FLOW's approach — just the brand name in a clean font on a dark background. This is the most scalable, recognizable, and professional look.

```
┌──────────────────────┐
│                      │
│    You Must Pray.    │
│                      │
│   [dark background]  │
│   [white/gold text]  │

│    sans-serif font]  │

```

### Where to Create It:
- **Canva** (free) — Search "Instagram profile picture" template, customize
- **Font recommendations:** Playfair Display (serif, elegant), Montserrat Bold (sans-serif, modern), or Cormorant Garamond (refined, spiritual feel)

## 4. STORY HIGHLIGHTS (Set Up BEFORE First Post)

Story Highlights are the "menu" of your page. They tell new visitors what your page is about in 2 seconds. Set these up with empty placeholder covers BEFORE you start posting.

### Recommended Highlights Structure:

| # | Highlight Name | Content | Icon/Cover |
|---|---------------|---------|------------|
| 1 | PRAY | Prayer-focused sermon clips & declarations | Praying hands or flame icon |
| 2 | WORD | Scripture breakdowns, teaching moments | Open Bible icon |
| 3 | SHIFT | Most powerful sermon moments (best of) | Lightning bolt or arrow icon |
| 4 | ABOUT | Who is Bishop Dag Heward-Mills, what is Flow Prayer Meeting | Info icon or cross |
| 5 | LIVE | Replays or announcements for live streams | Play button icon |

### Highlight Cover Design:
- **Same dark background** as your profile picture (brand consistency)
- **Simple white icon** on each cover
- **Text optional** — the highlight name below already labels it
- Create all 5 covers in Canva using the same template, just change the icon
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (standard story size, Instagram will crop to circle)

## 5. ACCOUNT SETTINGS OPTIMIZATION

### Switch to Professional Account
1. Go to Settings > Account > Switch to Professional Account
2. Select **Creator** (NOT Business — Creator gets better music library and Reels features)
3. Category: **Religious Organization** or **Public Figure**
4. This unlocks: Instagram Insights, contact button, content scheduling

### Other Settings:
- **Turn ON** suggested accounts (Settings > Privacy > Account Suggestions) — this puts you in the "Suggested for You" feed when similar accounts are followed
- **Turn OFF** private account — you MUST be public
- **Turn ON** Reels and story sharing — let people share your content
- **Connect to Facebook Page** (create one for "You Must Pray" if you don't have one) — this unlocks cross-posting and broader distribution

- [ ] Following 30-50 strategic accounts (Christian content pages, Dag Heward-Mills official, Lighthouse Chapel, similar sermon clip pages)
- [ ] 10 pieces of content READY in drafts before posting the first one

### Why 10 pieces ready before posting?
- If your first Reel goes viral, new visitors check your profile. If they see 1 post, they won't follow. If they see 5-10 quality posts, they follow immediately.
- Bank content so you never have to post under pressure
- Shows the algorithm you're a consistent creator from day 1

## 7. CONTENT VISUAL IDENTITY (Brand Consistency)

Every piece of content should be instantly recognizable as "You Must Pray" even without the username visible.

### Color Palette:
| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Black/Dark | #0A0A0A | Backgrounds, overlays |
| White | #FFFFFF | Primary text, subtitles |
| Gold | #D4A843 | Accent highlights, key words |
| Deep Red | #8B0000 | Power words, conviction moments |

### Typography:
- **Headlines/Hooks:** Bold, ALL CAPS, sans-serif (Impact, Bebas Neue, or Oswald)
- **Subtitles:** Clean, readable, medium weight (Montserrat or Inter)
- **Scripture references:** Italic or lighter weight for contrast

### Watermark:
Add a small "You Must Pray." watermark or "YMP" in the corner of every Reel:
- Bottom right corner
- Semi-transparent white
- Small but legible
- This brands your content even when shared/reposted without credit
