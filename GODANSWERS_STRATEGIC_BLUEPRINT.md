## 3. CONTENT PILLAR SYSTEM

Every post must fall into one of these 5 pillars. This trains the algorithm AND builds a loyal audience.

### Pillar 1: THE SHIFT (40% of content)
**Purpose:** Viral reach — these are designed to reach NON-followers
**Format:** Reels (15-45 seconds)
**What it is:** The single most powerful 15-30 second moment from a sermon. The clip that makes someone stop scrolling. Bold statement, emotional delivery, unexpected truth.
**Hook types:**
- "This truth will set you free..."
- Bold text overlay: "STOP RUNNING FROM GOD"
- Start mid-sentence at the peak emotional moment

**Example topics from Dag Heward-Mills sermons:**
- Breaking generational curses
- The cost of disobedience
- Why your season hasn't changed
- What happens when you stop praying

### Pillar 2: THE WORD (25% of content)
**Purpose:** Authority & depth — builds trust with existing followers
**Format:** Carousels (5-7 slides) or longer Reels (60-90 seconds)
**What it is:** A sermon teaching broken down into digestible points. Educational. Scripture-backed. Slide-by-slide breakdown.
**Structure for carousels:**
- Slide 1: Bold hook/question (e.g., "Why does God allow waiting seasons?")
- Slides 2-5: Key points from the sermon with scripture
- Slide 6: Summary/takeaway
- Slide 7: CTA ("Save this. Share with someone who needs it.")

### Pillar 3: THE CONVICTION (15% of content)
**Purpose:** Shares & saves — content people send to friends
**Format:** Reels with bold text overlays
**What it is:** Short, punchy declarations. The kind of 10-15 second clip that hits so hard people share it to their stories.
**Examples:**
- "ENOUGH IS ENOUGH" (like FLOW's pinned post)
- "This curse ends in YOUR generation"
- "You've been playing with God's patience"
- "Stop dating people who don't pray"

### Pillar 4: THE INVITATION (10% of content)
**Purpose:** Community building & CTA-driven content
**Format:** Story posts, event promos, live stream announcements
**What it is:** Content that directs people to take action — attend a service, join a live, visit the church, share their testimony.

### Pillar 5: THE TESTIMONY (10% of content)
**Purpose:** Social proof & emotional connection
**Format:** Text-on-screen Reels, screenshot DMs (with permission), before/after stories
**What it is:** Stories of transformation. What happened when someone applied the teaching. This creates emotional investment and community.

## 4. VIRAL REELS FORMULA

### The You Must Pray Reel Structure (15-45 seconds)

```
SECOND 0-2:   HOOK (text overlay + first words)
              Must create tension or curiosity.
              "The reason your prayers aren't being answered..."
              "God told me to tell you something today..."
              "You need to hear this before it's too late..."

SECOND 2-15:  DELIVERY (the sermon moment)
              The raw, powerful clip. Minimal editing.
              Let the preaching speak.

SECOND 15-30: PEAK (the punch line / climax)
              The moment that creates the emotional reaction.
              This is where shares happen.

SECOND 30-45: RESOLVE (optional — landing the message)
              Only if the clip needs a conclusion.
              Many viral clips END at the peak — no resolve.
```

### Editing Rules:
1. **Subtitles on EVERY video** — 85% of Instagram is watched on mute
2. **Bold keyword highlights** — Key words in yellow/white, rest in standard color
3. **Dark or cinematic color grade** — Premium feel, not amateur
4. **NO long intros** — Start at the powerful moment, not 30 seconds before it
5. **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical ALWAYS
6. **Audio:** Clean, clear sermon audio. Add subtle background music ONLY if it enhances (ambient pads, not distracting worship music)
7. **Text overlays:** Use bold, sans-serif fonts. ALL CAPS for power statements.

## ABSOLUTE RULES — NON-NEGOTIABLE

### CONTENT STANDARDS
1. **Generic content is FORBIDDEN** — Every post must be specific, intentional, and engineered for a strategic outcome. No filler. No "just posting to post."
2. **Posting without intention is FORBIDDEN** — Before EVERY post, answer: "What is the strategic purpose of this post?" If you can't answer clearly, don't post it.
3. **Copying trends without logic is FORBIDDEN** — A trend is only used if it aligns with the brand, serves the audience, and has a clear strategic reason. Trend-jacking for views with no brand connection = wasted reach.
4. **Every post includes the strategic WHY** — In content planning docs, every piece of content must have a documented reason: growth, authority, saves, shares, conversion, or community.
5. **Think like a professional Social Media Manager** — Not a random creator. Every decision is data-informed, brand-aligned, and audience-first.

### CTA PHILOSOPHY
6. **Every CTA must be SHOCKING, VIRAL, and PATTERN-BREAKING** — No "like and follow for more." No generic CTAs. Every CTA must:
   - **Stop the scroll** — Use unexpected language, bold declarations, or direct commands
   - **Create an emotional reaction** — Guilt, conviction, urgency, hope, or defiance
   - **Demand action** — Not suggest. DEMAND.

### CTA Examples (You Must Pray Standard):
| FORBIDDEN (Generic) | REQUIRED (Pattern-Breaking) |
|---------------------|----------------------------|
| "Like and follow for more" | "If you scrolled past this without sharing, ask yourself why." |
| "Comment amen below" | "Tag someone who stopped praying. This is their wake-up call." |
| "Share if you agree" | "Send this to someone before midnight. They need this word TODAY." |
| "Turn on notifications" | "If you're seeing this, God didn't let the algorithm hide it from you. Follow before He has to remind you twice." |
| "Double tap if you love God" | "Screenshot this. Put it on your wall. Pray it every morning for 7 days. Then come back and tell me what God did." |
| "Follow for daily inspiration" | "You just watched this. Now you have two choices: scroll and forget, or share it and plant a seed. Choose." |

### WHY This Approach Works:
- **Generic CTAs get ignored** — People have seen "like and follow" 10,000 times. Their brain skips it.
- **Pattern-breaking CTAs create TENSION** — They force the viewer to make a choice. That tension drives action.
- **Conviction-based CTAs align with the brand** — "You Must Pray" is already a command. The CTAs must carry that same energy.
- **Shocking CTAs get SHARED** — When someone reads "If you scrolled past this without sharing, ask yourself why" — they share it. The CTA itself becomes content.
